About us

Since the early beginnings we at AGM were a customer-centric Company doing our utmost personally & professionally to meet & exceed our Customer's expectations. This simple rule was & still is our main success factor, all our developments through the years based on it. Taking the company from phase to another creating international partnerships with the most Respected Global players in our industry with the efforts exerted by our team members whom are the most valuable asset of all time. Since 1980 & through the different growth phases, AGM developed its purpose, vision and mission to meet the changing needs of our stakeholders adding to that our corporate social responsibilities by adding value to the community.
